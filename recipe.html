Recipe 1: Goan Veg Thali


Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Serving: 4 servings

Ingredients: &

  • Vegetable Curry: 2 tbsp coconut oil, 1 onion, 1 tomato, 1 potato, 1 carrot, 1/2 cup cauliflower, 1/2 cup peas, 1/2 cup coconut milk, spices, salt, coriander leaves. Dal: 1 cup toor dal, mustard seeds, red chilies, 1 onion, 1 tomato, spices, salt, coriander leaves. Chapati: 1 cup whole wheat flour, water, salt, ghee. Rice: 1 cup basmati rice, water, ghee, salt. Coconut Chutney: 1/2 cup grated coconut, 1 green chili, ginger, cumin seeds, salt, water

Instructions:

  1. Vegetable Curry: Sauté onions in coconut oil, add tomato, then veggies, spices, and coconut milk. Simmer for 10-15 mins. Dal: Cook toor dal, then temper with mustard seeds, chilies, onions, and tomatoes. Simmer for 5 mins. Chapati: Mix flour, salt, and water to form dough. Roll into circles and cook on a tawa with ghee. Rice: Boil rice with water, ghee, and salt until tender. Chutney: Blend coconut, green chili, ginger, cumin seeds, salt, and water into a smooth paste.

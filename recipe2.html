Recipe 2: Mutton Biryani


Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Cooking Time: 1hour

Serving: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 500g mutton, 2 cups basmati rice, 2 tbsp ghee, 2 onions, 2 tomatoes, 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste, spices (turmeric, cumin, garam masala), 1/2 cup yogurt, 1/4 cup coriander, 1/4 cup mint, 2-3 cloves, cardamom, cinnamon stick, salt, lemon juice.

Instructions:

  1. Cook Rice: Soak and cook rice 70% done with spices. Drain, Cook Mutton: Sauté onions in ghee, add ginger-garlic, tomatoes, mutton, and spices. Cook until tender. Stir in yogurt, Layer Biryani: Layer mutton, rice, mint, coriander, fried onions. Drizzle with lemon juice and ghee, Cook Biryani: Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, Serve: Garnish with fried onions and fresh herbs. Enjoy!.

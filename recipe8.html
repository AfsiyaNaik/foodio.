Recipe 8: Crispy Dosa


Preparation Time: 8-10 hours (for soaking and fermenting)

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Serving: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Water, as needed for batter
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice and urad dal separately in water. Soak them in enough water along with fenugreek seeds for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  2. After soaking, drain the rice and dal. In a wet grinder or blender, grind the rice and dal together into a smooth batter, adding water as needed to achieve a smooth and slightly thick consistency.
  3. Transfer the batter to a large bowl, cover it, and let it ferment for 8-10 hours or overnight in a warm place. The batter should rise and become slightly bubbly after fermenting.
  4. Once the batter is fermented, add salt and stir the batter gently.
  5. Heat a non-stick pan or dosa tawa over medium heat. Once hot, drizzle a little oil or ghee on the pan and spread it evenly.
  6. Pour a ladle of the batter onto the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  7. Cook the dosa until the edges turn golden brown and crispy. You can drizzle a little more oil or ghee on the sides for extra crispiness.
  8. Once the dosa is cooked, fold it and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar. Enjoy your crispy dosa!

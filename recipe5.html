Recipe 5: Crispy Samosa


Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Serving: 6 servings

Ingredients:

  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup boiled peas
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 6 samosa wrappers (or spring roll wrappers)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, and sauté for another 2 minutes.
  3. Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Stir well to combine.
  4. Mix in boiled mashed potatoes and peas, and cook for a few minutes while stirring. Remove from heat and add chopped cilantro. Let the filling cool.
  5. Take a samosa wrapper or spring roll wrapper, and fold it into a cone shape. Fill the cone with the potato-pea filling, leaving the top open.
  6. Seal the open edge with a little water to form a triangle or a cone. Repeat for the rest of the wrappers.
  7. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying. Fry the samosas in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per batch.
  8. Drain excess oil and serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce. Enjoy your crispy samosas!

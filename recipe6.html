Recipe 6: Smoky Grilled Chicken


Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 25 minutes

Serving: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • 1-2 wood chips for smoking (optional, for extra smokiness)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, dried thyme, black pepper, and salt. Mix well to create the marinade.
  2. Place the chicken breasts in the marinade and coat them evenly. Cover and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes (or up to 1 hour for more flavor).
  3. If using a grill, preheat it to medium-high heat. If using a charcoal grill, add soaked wood chips to create smoke (optional for smoky flavor).
  4. Place the marinated chicken on the grill and cook for about 6-8 minutes per side, or until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C) and has a nice golden-brown color.
  5. Remove the chicken from the grill and let it rest for a few minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with your favorite side dishes. Enjoy your smoky grilled chicken!
